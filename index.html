About KALAJNA

Immerse yourself in the exciting national-level cultural and literary celebration known as KALAJNA, hosted by NGSMIPS, Nitte (DU). This event is a vibrant platform for students to showcase their artistic and literary talents, blending the richness of India's culture with the spirit of academic growth. Students from across the nation gather here, not only to celebrate creativity but also to experience the charm of Mangalore – from its scenic beaches to historic temples and lush greenery. With performances that promote unity and pride in our culture, Kalajna gives everyone a chance to appreciate our country's diversity. Our goal is to honour traditions, inspire creativity and encourage intercultural learning. This festival is the right place to explore different art forms, traditions, and styles of expression. Come, join us at KALAJNA…

About the Event

Join us for the grand "Harmony Extravaganza," where the best of Indian Folk singing will captivate you with vibrant performances. Participate in exciting solo, duet, and group events!

Sub Events

Participants

Maximum 2 groups/Institute

Team Size

Minimum 4/Group

Time Limit

5 min + 1 min (sound check)

Rules and Regulations

  • Teams may choose to perform with a karaoke track or use any of the following provided instruments: acoustic guitar, drum kit, synthesizer, and tabla.
  • Additional instruments brought by the team are permitted; however, only a maximum of three instruments may be played simultaneously during the performance.

  • Props and accessories that enhance the performance are allowed, provided they are relevant to the song and do not distract from the performance.
  • All decisions made by the judges are final and are not open to debate or appeal.

Judging Criteria

Vocals (30%), Harmony (30%), Stage Presence (20%), Overall Impact (20%)

Antakshari

A Musical Showdown

About the Event

Step into the world of melody and rhythm! Antakshari is all about keeping the chain of songs flowing, with the challenge of matching each song with the last letter or syllable of the previous one. Who will be the maestro?

Event Details

Participants

1 Group/Institute, Team size: 3 members

Time Limit

Written prelims followed by finals

Rules and Regulations

  • Written prelims followed by finals. The top 6 teams enter the finals.
  • Each team must sing a song starting with the last letter or syllable of the previous song.
  • Teams must start their song within a fixed time limit. Failure to do so results in losing that turn.
  • No song can be repeated once it has been sung during the event.
  • All songs must be in Hindi.
  • Any changes to the event format will be communicated in advance.

Judging Criteria

The event will be judged based on the flow of the songs, adherence to the theme, timing, and creativity in song choices.

Debate

Sharpen Your Arguments and Articulate Your Ideas!

About the Event

Engage in a thought-provoking debate where critical thinking, argumentation, and the ability to defend both sides of an issue will be tested. Debate is an opportunity to showcase your eloquence, reasoning, and debating skills!

Event Details

Participants

1 Groups/Institute

Team Size

2 Members

No. of Rounds

1

Rules and Regulations

  • Participants will select a theme from a pool of five and receive a motion related to their chosen theme.
  • The topic will be revealed 15 minutes before the presentation.
  • Participants will be assigned to either the proposition or opposition side initially and will switch sides for the second part of the debate.
  • Each participant speaks for 2 minutes in favor of the motion, 2 minutes against it, followed by a 1-minute conclusion.
  • Speeches must be delivered in English.

Judging Criteria

Participants will be judged based on:

  • Capability to articulate well-organized and coherent arguments on both perspectives (30%)
  • Receptiveness to opposing arguments (30%)
  • General proficiency in debating skills and expression (40%)

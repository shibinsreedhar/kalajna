About KALAJNA

Immerse yourself in the exciting national-level cultural and literary celebration known as KALAJNA, hosted by NGSMIPS, Nitte (DU). This event is a vibrant platform for students to showcase their artistic and literary talents, blending the richness of India's culture with the spirit of academic growth. Students from across the nation gather here, not only to celebrate creativity but also to experience the charm of Mangalore – from its scenic beaches to historic temples and lush greenery. With performances that promote unity and pride in our culture, Kalajna gives everyone a chance to appreciate our country's diversity. Our goal is to honour traditions, inspire creativity and encourage intercultural learning. This festival is the right place to explore different art forms, traditions, and styles of expression. Come, join us at KALAJNA…

Rules & Regulations

Please read the rules and regulations carefully before registering for the event. Violation of any rule may lead to disqualification.

Institute Participation

The original permission letter from the participating institute along with the copy of government issued identity cards should be submitted at the registration desk before the commencement of the fest.

Event Registration

A participant can register for a maximum of three events.

Disqualification

The college will not be eligible for the overall championship if their team has been disqualified from any event.

Event Submissions

Submission of any information related to events (e.g., music, photographs) must be provided in a pen drive before the event commences.

Reporting Time

All participants must report at the venue at least one hour prior to the commencement of the event.

Alcohol and Substance Use

Consumption of alcohol, narcotics, and other illegal substances anywhere on campus is strictly prohibited.

Organizers' Rights

Organizers hold the right to change event rules before the event begins without prior notice.

Responsibility

Organizers are not responsible for any loss or damage to participants' personal belongings.

Violation

Any participant found violating the rules may be immediately expelled from the campus and disqualified from all events.
